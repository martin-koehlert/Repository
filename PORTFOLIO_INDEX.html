OTHER

My name is Martin Koehlert and I am an artist from southern New Hampshire. I mainly work in Graphic Design but I enjoy all aspects of art and enjoy Printmaking, Illustration, and Painting as a hobby. I believe that every media can be useful for a graphic designer because incorporating these styles into the work gives it a sense uniqueness and personality. Some of my most successful work came from incorporating my other hobbies into the design. This is why I am dedicated to learning as many different medias and styles as possible, so I can further enhance my work.
